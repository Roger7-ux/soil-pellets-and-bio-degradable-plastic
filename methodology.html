Project Methodology

The project follows two independent preparation processes followed by evaluation.

Soil pH Balancing Pellets

  • Collected and cleaned waste eggshells
  • Dried and ground into fine powder
  • Powdered waste chalk separately
  • Mixed eggshell & chalk powders uniformly
  • Added natural binder and water
  • Shaped mixture into pellets
  • Dried pellets for stability

Biodegradable Plastic

  • Prepared starch-water slurry
  • Added glycerin as plasticizer
  • Heated with continuous stirring
  • Formed transparent viscous solution
  • Poured and spread thinly
  • Allowed to cool and dry
  • Obtained biodegradable plastic film
